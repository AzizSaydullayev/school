Teachers are professional, inspiring, and caring mentors who, with sincere dedication to their work, help each student unlock their potential, instilling a love for knowledge and confidence in their abilities.

In our school, there are computer labs, Uzbek language classrooms, a gym, a field, a labor education classroom, and many other classrooms for various subjects.

In our school, various events are often held in the assembly hall, dedicated to holidays and important occasions. Teachers and students work together to prepare theatrical performances, concerts, competitions, and sports events, creating an atmosphere of celebration and unity. These events help develop creative skills, strengthen friendships, and create positive memories for all participants.

In our school, there is a large and bright cafeteria with a cozy atmosphere, where incredibly delicious and diverse dishes are prepared every day, pleasing all the students and creating a great mood, helping to restore energy after classes and enjoy lunch with friends.

In our school, there is a cozy library where students can take books and enjoy reading. During the break, many of them come here to dive into the world of literature, take interesting books with them, and spend their time productively, expanding their horizons and developing their imagination.

On Saturdays, our school organizes cleaning activities where students and teachers work together to keep the school tidy. Students help sweep the hallways, clean classrooms, and maintain the cleanliness of the school grounds, fostering a sense of responsibility and care for their environment.
